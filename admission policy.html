Admission Policies And Procedures

To be eligible for admission into any NAF post primary school
a child must:
  • Be between the age of 10 years and above.
  • have completed primary six by the year of admission.
  • sit for and pass the the entrance examination.
  • Be medically fit.
the admission procedure includes:
  • purchasing the entrance exam forms when advertised.
  • Filling the forms on-line where the exam date and venues as well as choice of school are indicated.
  • Sitting for the entrance examination.
  • Honouring the invitation for written and oral interview through letter written to that effect (for sucessful candidates).
  • Receiving admission letters containig material requirements issued to sucessful candidates.
  • Admitted candidates reporting to their respective schools
it is also the practice that:
  • Children are admitted from all the state of the federation and the Federal Capital Teritoty
  • Admission is open to all Nigerian children who meet the criteria irrespective of their parental background
