The Nigeria Airforce directorate of education Logo

The logo interpretation

The eagle perching on the national flag is cropped from the Nigerian Airforce Logo because everything has to be done in sync with services wide
goals and aspirations.
The five star signify NAF educational institutions five star status. nothing but stellar performances is expected from them in all activities they engage in.
the burning candle indicates that teachers are like candles,burn to illuminates the path for others to see clearly.
The closed book indicates the fountain of knowledge. Seekers of knowledge make efforts to open the book.
The flowers indicate the budding and blossoming stage of the learners as they are being groomed to take on the world of work and be part of tutored solutions to societal problems.
Every activity of the directorate is anchored on knowledge,intergrity and excellence
